Diversity, equity and inclusion

I have been deeply tied to many diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) efforts. Here's a few of them.

  • Athlete Empowerment Coalition

    I am the co-founder and chair of an official student organization at Mount Holyoke College called the Athlete Empowerment Coalition. The AEC provides a community for student-athletes of color and first-generation student-athletes, brought together by a shared love of sports.

  • Student-Athlete Advisory Committee

    I was one of two reperesentatives for the rowing team during the 2019, 2020, and 2021 calendar years. In 2020 and 2021, I was the first chair of diversity, equity, and inclusion on SAAC and led the successful vote to create a new DEI subcommittee.

  • A Most Beautiful Thing

    I hosted a virtual panel with 150+ attendees, and interviewed award-winning filmmaker and Olympic rower Mary Mazzio, as well as author of A Most Beautiful Thing Arshay Cooper. A Most Beautiful Thing is a documentary film and memoir about the first all-Black rowing team in the nation.

  • Trans Athletes

    I have been featured on NBC News twice speaking about legislation targeting transgender athletes, particularly transgender women. [Video 1] [Video 2]

  • Queer Prom

    I organized the first three Bay Area Queer Proms. Every year, I raised $3000, booked a venue, and successfully organized and hosted 200+ people. This has become an annual event.
